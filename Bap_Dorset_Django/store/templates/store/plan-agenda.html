    Agenda

    As May is the month dedicated to mental health, we have obviously chosen to organize our festival during this month.

    Fri.

    19th

    • Concert - 1pm to 3pm - location 2

    • A concert of Selena Gomez will be the oppenning of the festival. Being an artist that talked a lot about anxiety we thaught that she could really speak to the people on a more personnal level. We hope you can be there!

    • Guided meditation - 5pm to 7.30pm- location 3

    • During ou entire festival, we will have dedicated meditation zones that would be open all the time but we thaught having a guided session at the beginning of the festival to peak the interest of the people that never tried it, but still want to learn about it.

    • Film - 8pm - location 5

    • To end our first day we will all have the opportunity to watch a movie with every-one and relax before leaving the festival. We want every on to leave the festival with a sense of peace and calm before going to sleep.
